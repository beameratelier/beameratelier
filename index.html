About Beamer Atelier

Beamer Atelier is a design studio dedicated to teaching-first LaTeX Beamer themes. Our work is grounded in the belief that presentation design should support thinking, explanation, and sustained attention—especially in academic and professional teaching contexts.

Rather than prioritising visual novelty, Beamer Atelier focuses on structure, navigational clarity, and typographic discipline. Each theme is built as a stable visual system, intended to recede behind the content while quietly guiding audiences through complex material.

The themes are designed for long-form lectures, research presentations, and executive teaching, where clarity, consistency, and cognitive ease matter more than decoration.
